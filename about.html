About

I am Sona, a Roblox gameplay and systems engineer focused on building practical, maintainable, and performant game systems in Luau.

My work sits between gameplay engineering, systems optimization, developer tooling, and production support. I enjoy turning messy constraints into simple, shippable solutions: improving inherited codebases, building reusable tools, tuning player-facing systems, and helping teams move faster without sacrificing reliability.

My engineering style is influenced by data-oriented design and the belief that simpler systems are easier to ship, debug, teach, and extend.

Outside of programming, I compose music for games and experimental media, which has shaped how I think about atmosphere, pacing, and player experience.
